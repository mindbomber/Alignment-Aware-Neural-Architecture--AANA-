# Papers

This folder contains public research manuscripts connected to the AANA evaluation pipeline.

## AANA framework manuscript

- `aana-framework.pdf` - Alignment-Aware Neural Architectures (AANA): Constraint-Coherent Learning via Verifier-Grounded Correction.

Note: this is an early research manuscript. It includes theoretical framing, architecture design, evaluation protocol, and simulated comparative results. Treat it as exploratory research context, not as a peer-reviewed benchmark claim.

## Companion theory paper

- `invisible-divergence-layered-alignment-dynamics.pdf` - Invisible Divergence and Layered Alignment Dynamics: A Constraint-Based Theory of Capability, Correction, and Viable Design.

Note: this paper frames the capability-alignment gap, invisible divergence, hidden constraint layers, and pressure-sensitive alignment dynamics that motivate the evaluation pipeline. Its pilot-style results are illustrative design validation, not final model-run evidence.
